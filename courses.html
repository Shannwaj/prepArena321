Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have access to the course?

Once enrolled, you will have lifetime access to the course materials. You can study at your own pace and revisit the content whenever needed.

Are there any prerequisites for these courses?

Most courses are designed to accommodate learners at all levels. However, some advanced courses may require basic prerequisite knowledge, which will be clearly mentioned in the course description.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with the content, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase.

How are the mock tests conducted?

Mock tests are conducted online through our platform. They are designed to simulate the actual exam environment and provide detailed performance analysis and feedback.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes, for career-oriented courses like coding interview preparation and data science, we provide placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and connections with our hiring partners.

Can I download the course materials for offline study?

Yes, most of our study materials, including PDFs, practice questions, and notes, can be downloaded for offline access. Video lectures are available for streaming only.
