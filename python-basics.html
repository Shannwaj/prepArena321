Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior programming experience?

No, this course is designed for absolute beginners. We start from the very basics and gradually progress to more advanced topics.

What software do I need for this course?

You'll need Python installed on your computer (we show you how), and any text editor or IDE. We recommend using Visual Studio Code or PyCharm (free version).

How long do I have access to the course?

Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to the course materials. You can learn at your own pace and revisit lectures whenever you need.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

Yes, you'll receive a certificate of completion once you finish all the modules and projects. This can be added to your resume or LinkedIn profile.

How much time should I dedicate to this course?

We recommend spending 3-5 hours per week. The entire course can be completed in about 8 weeks with this pace, but you're free to learn at your own speed.

Can I download the course materials for offline viewing?

Yes, all lecture notes and exercise files can be downloaded for offline use. Video lectures are available for streaming only.
